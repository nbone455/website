History and Future of Evolutionary Biology

The convergence of past contributors in the field, their cultural context, and the resulting assumptions, biases, and inferences that shape evolutionary thinking has always captivated me. I believe that fully acknowledging and reconciling this historical legacy with the present is crucial for advancing evolutionary research beyond the constraints of systemic racism, eugenics, and unjustified discrimination. I hope to eventually expand my interest in this convergence into a more formal research effort.
