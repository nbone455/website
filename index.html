About Me

Hi, I'm Nic Bone. I'm currently a PhD candidate in the Uyeda Lab at Virginia Tech. I'm interested in capturing the attributes of hard-to-measure traits for phylogenetic comparative methods, with my dissertation focused specifically on modelling the evolution of avian migration (see my research page for this and other projects).
